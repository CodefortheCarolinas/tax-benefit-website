Get Your Child Tax Credit

Families are eligible for thousands of dollars in Child Tax Credit and other tax benefits this year. A low-income family of four who didn’t file last year could get nearly $20,000 in tax benefits — but only if they file a return.

Research shows that state and local benefits agencies are the best messengers to reach out to families who don’t usually file tax returns. A single text message to SNAP beneficiaries in a medium-sized state could get over 1,000 families to claim millions of dollars in benefits.
